Introduction

Goals

  • Expose the [[Description]] internal slot of a symbol directly instead of just indirectly through `Symbol.prototype.toString`.

get Symbol.prototype.description

`Symbol.prototype.description` is an accessor property whose set accessor function is *undefined*. Its get accessor function performs the following steps:

1. Let _s_ be the *this* value. 1. If Type(_s_) is Symbol, let _sym_ be _s_. 1. Else, 1. If Type(_s_) is not Object, throw a *TypeError* exception. 1. If _s_ does not have a [[SymbolData]] internal slot, throw a *TypeError* exception. 1. Let _sym_ be _s_.[[SymbolData]]. 1. Return _sym_.[[Description]].
